About [ Mogok ]
Mogok, known as ruby land is greatly known for its Gemstones such as Ruby, Sapphire, and Jade stone. The tourist is attracted by the unique views of the breathtaking Ruby Land. Also, the Gem trade is wide in Mogok. Some entrepreneurs are investigating the market.
The roads leading to Mogok have a lot of turns, so it's not very convenient for people who get dizzy in a car while driving. There are total999 turns, and if you go to Mogok from Yangon, you have to drive for 11 hours. There is no airport in Mogok. However, if you fly from Yangon to Mandalay, and drive Mandalay to Mogok it will only take about 5 hours.
Mogok has gem mines that tourists can visit to explore the process of how are rubies from tunnels and square mines. There are plenty of photography spots for avid nature photographers. There are also many shops in the village. Who loves walking, it's a perfect place to come to the village. The chilly climate makes it comfortable to walk around the village.
It's a gem market, the most popular work in Mogok. You should buy some cuz they're so beautiful.
