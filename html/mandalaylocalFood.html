Local Food [ Mandalay ]
Htoe Mont and La Mote are the most popular foods in not only Mandalay but also upper Myanmar. They are sweet and tasty. The main ingredients are glutinous rice, raisins, cashews, desiccated coconuts, and rice flour.
Meeshay is the famous food in Mandalay. It is very tasty and even foreigners try it. It is also easy to make and not too cost. You may try this food when you visit Inle.
Wat Thar Dote Htoe is very popular in Mandalay. It is very tasty and not very expensive. You may get it in some street food shops.
