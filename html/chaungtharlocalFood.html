Local Food [ Chaung Tha ]
Fried Shrimp is the most popular seafood in Chaung Thar. It is crispy and very perfect with the sauce. You may see them sold in stalls or by children on foot.
Seafood salad is one of the most famous food in Chaung Thar. It is very tasty and alittle bit expensive. You may find them in some restaurants and hotels.
Coconut juice is popular in not only Chaung Thar but also Myanmar. It is sweet and not very expensive. It is easy to find anywhere in Chaung Thar.
