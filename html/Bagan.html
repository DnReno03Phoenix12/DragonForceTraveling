About Bagan

Bagan's attractions

Bagan is a historical ancient city and the oldest city in Myanmar. Bagan is located in Mandalay, Myanmar. Bagan is a UNESCO member country. There are mnay famous temples built by ancient kings. Puppets Burmese crafts, Burmese musical instruments, Burmese cultural items can be found in Bagan.

Bagan's Local Food

There are many traditional foods and curries in Bagan that you may try when you visit Bagan. Such as, Phone Yay Gyi, Tea salad, Sugarcane Juice.
