About [ Mandalay ]
Mandalay, well known for its many religious attractions for Buddhism, is the second-largest city in Myanmar. It is located on the east bank of the Irrawaddy River and the city has a population of 1400,000.
It is fine to go to Mandalay in different ways, by train, coach, plane, or ship. There are various travel agencies to arrange a trip to Mandalay. There are many of traffic congestion in the afternoon because of the crowded places in Mandalay.
You may visit Mandalay any time of the whole year but the best time is between October and January. There are many enjoyable festivals for people during this period. So, Mandalay is full of crowded areas at those times.
Mandalay is divided into seven townships- Amarapura, Aungmyethazan, Chanayethazan, Chanmyathazi, Maha Aungmye, Patheingyi, and Pyigyidagun.
To get around Mandalay, you can hire a tuk-tuk, taxi, or buses to visit any places of interest of your choice. You can also go around by private cars with tour guides.
Tuk-tuk ride is enjoyable. You can go around with the vehicle. For visitors, there are souvenirs galore sold in stalls in the big market(Zay Cho market).
Mandalay is famous for its many religious attractions for Buddhism. It's a great city to explore for Buddhists and those who wish to know more about religion.
Mandalay is famous for its history, art and culture, and pagodas and temples. They are worth visiting for the many interesting places which are abundant in Mandalay.
