About [ Tain Pin Lae ]
Tain Pin Lae, well known as the sea of clouds, is the most beautiful iconic place in Shan State with natural beauty. It is a famous place and most of the visitors are visiting these days. You may see the fog in front you your eyes. It is located in Shan State, near Naung Cho.
It is fine to visit to Tain Pin Lae by bike, car, or motorbike. There are not any various travel agencies to arrange a trip to Tain Pin Lae. The transportation is not very good but you may see the beautiful views to fresh your mind and make you relax.
You can visit Tain Pin Lae in the winter (November to January) which is the coldest time of the year. You will see a lot of full of fog at those periods.
Tain Pin Lae is the most beautiful place in Shan State because of its foggy views from the top of the mountain slope.
To get around Tain Pin Lae, you can hire a coach and private cars to visit to Tain Pin Lae. You can hire hotels in Tain Pin Lae. But the costs are extremely expensive. You can enjoy the beauty of nature in Tain Pin Lae.
Tain Pin Lae is famous for its scenic views from the top of the hills and is very beautiful.
