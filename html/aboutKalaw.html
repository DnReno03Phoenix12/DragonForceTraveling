About [ Kalaw ]
Kalaw, well known as Myanmar's trekking mecca, which is the most pleasant and beautiful city in Myanmar. It is located in the township of Taunggyi District high up in the southern Shan State of Myanmar. The population of Kalaw is about 200,000.
It is great to go to Kalaw in different ways, by Express, coach, plane, or car. There are various travel agencies to arrange a trip to Kalaw. There is not much traffic congestion in Kalaw because of its less population and because it is a hill town.
You may visit Kalaw any time of the season but the best time is between December and February. The weather is very cool and this time of year in Kalaw is enjoyable for warm-weather travelers. There are many enjoyable activities for people during this period.
There are many places of interest in Kalaw such as colorful flower-lined streets, coffee and tea plantations, elephant camps, the Shwe U Min Pagoda, and the Palaung village. The 'Big Market Day" is one of the famous places in Kalaw which is held on a five-day cycle. The Palaung people grow tea, damsons, and mangoes on the hillsides. Two enjoyable outdoor activities in Kalaw are hiking and camping around the town and up in the hills.
To get around Kalaw, you can go around by train, taxi & shuttle, and bus. You can also go around in private cars of your own with your family.
Hiking and camping are the most enjoyable outdoor activities. For visitors, there are souvenirs galore sold in stalls on Big Market Day.
Kalaw is famous for its cool climate and scenic views. It is also known as Pine Land. And the air is fresh, carrying the sweet smell of the pine trees. It's a great hill town to relax for visitors and tourists.
Kalaw is famous for its cool climate and scenic views. They are worth visiting for the many interesting places which are abundant and outdoor activities in Kalaw.
