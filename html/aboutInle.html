About [ Inle Lake ]
Inle is well known as the heart of Shan State, which is about 900 m above sea level and 22km long. It is about 39km away from the capital city of Shan State. Mountain ranges that surround the area are reflected on the lake's surface, offering an impressive view.
It is fine to go to Inle lake in different ways, by train, coach, car, or boat. here are various travel agencies to arrange a trip to Inle. You will see there are mountain ranges and green valleys surrounding the lake and houses and famous pagodas built on the surface of the lake.
You may visit Inle any time of the year but the best time is between November to February which is during the dry and cool season. There are many enjoyable festivals for people during this period. So, Inle is full of crowded areas with visitors at that time.
You may visit the places of interest there. The iconic places in Inle are Ywama, Mine Thauk & Phaung Daw Oo, Nan Pan, Pan Thaung, and Taung To. Paung Daw Oo is one of the most famous pagodas in Inle full of Buddhism and tourists every day. Nan Pan market is also one of the biggest markets in Inle which is a 5-day rotating market.
To get around Inle, you can hire a motor boat, to visit any places of interest of your choice. The costs are not very big. Not only visitors but also tourists ride on the motor boat to get around Inle lake.
The Intha use their leg to ride a boat. This is also a famous tradition in Inle. You will see widely about Intha using their leg to ride boats and do fishing when you visit Inle.
Inle is famous for its floating villages and gardens and the unique way of life of the local people. It's a great place to gain a great deal of knowledge for tourists who wish to know about their traditions and religion.
