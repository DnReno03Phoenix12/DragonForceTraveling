Local Food [ Bagan ]
Most popular food in Bagan is "Pone Yay Gyi" The main ingredient is the bean. It can get in every restaurant in Bagan. Also, Tea leaf salad and Burmese curries are available in every restaurant. Some famous desserts are Glutinous Rice.
Tea salad is the traditional food of Bagan. It is very tasty and even foreigners try it. It is also easy to make and not too costly.
Sugarcane Juice is sweet and delicious. It can be found in famous pagodas and some street food shops. It doesn't cost too much and foreigners like its taste.
