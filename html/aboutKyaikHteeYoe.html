About [ Kyaik Htee Yoe ]
Kyaik Htee Yoe, well known as the huge golden rock perched at its summit, is located in Mon State. It is a small pagoda built on the top of a granite boulder covered with gold leaves pasted on by its male worshippers. It is also a tourist attractions full of visitors and tourists and famous in Myanmar. It is 3,600 feet above sea level and is located in Mon State.
It is fine to go to Kyaik Htee Yoe by bus, car, or coach. There are various travel agencies to arrange a trip to Kyaik Htee Yoe. You can also visit there in your own car.
You can visit Kyaik Htee Yoe any time of the whole year but the best time is in summer (March through May). There are many festivals held by the local people during that period.
There are many interesting places in Kyaik Htee Yoe such as Mount Kyaiktiyo, Kyaik Htee Yoe Pagoda and monasteries. Zipline vehicle ride is fun and famous among tourists and visitors.
To get around Kyaik Htee Yoe, you can hire taxis and cars to visit any places of interest of your choice. You can also go around by zipline vehicle to enjoy the beauty of Kyaik Htee Yoe.
Kyaik Htee Yoe is a well-known Buddhist pilgrimage site in Mon State,Myanmar. The view of the mountain ranges and green valley from the top of Mount Kyaik Htee Yoe is enjoyable and famous for visitors. You may also see souvenir shops sold in stalls near the mountain.
Kyaik Htee Yoe is famous for its cool climate and scenic views. They are worth visiting for the many interesting places which are abundant and outdoor activities in Mount Kyaik Htee Yoe.
